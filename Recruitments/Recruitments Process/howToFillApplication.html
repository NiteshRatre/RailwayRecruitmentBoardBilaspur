How to fill Application form

*Incomplete/illegible applications will lead to rejection of applications.

**Please go through the Notification thoroughly and carefully and fill up the form adhering to the instructions quoted therein. However, the following guidelines will assist to fill up the Application Form.

Employment Notice No. CEN – 01/2010

  • Serial No.
    Application phase
    Details
  • 1.
    Application form

    I. Fill up the category No. and Name of the post applied for compulsorily.

    II. No option is called for the column should be left blank.


    (II is applicable to CEN Nso.01/2010)

  • 2.
    Application form

    Give the option from 2 Railways viz. SECR or CR as per your choice/preference.

  • 3.
    Application form

    Please write the name as mentioned in Matriculation certificate/High School Certificate in capital letters

  • 4.
    Application form

    Tick the community to which you belong. Please ensure that the same community is filled up wherever it is asked for.

  • 5.
    Application form

    Please write Father’s name as mentioned in Matriculation certificate/High School Certificate in capital letters

  • 6.
    Application form

    The Date of birth should be entered in the application form as per the Matriculation certificate/High School Certificate or Equivalent certificate.

  • 7.
    Application form

    The age as on 01.07.2009 should be filled up. The upper age limit and lower age limit, as given in the notification shall be ensured.

  • 8.
    Application form

    Please indicate any two visible marks, on body, like a mole/scar etc.

  • 9.
    Application form

    Write academic/technical qualification duly filling all the details given in the form.

  • 10.
    Application form

    Kindly fill up the full address clearly indicating Village, Street No./House No. Tehsil, District, State as well as PIN CODE.

  • 11.
    Application form

    Please write the nearest Railway Station from your correspondence address in capital letters.

  • 12.
    Application form

    Please write the nearest Railway Station from your correspondence address in capital letters.

  • 13.
    Left hand thumb impression

    Please affix your Left Thumb Impression (LTI) in the space provided/specified for it, where the ridges must be clear and legible. LTI should not be blurred/smudged, dark or unreadable; else the application will be rejected.

  • 14.
    Signature

    Please put your full signature in running hand (NOT IN CAPITAL LETTERS) either in English or Hindi at the place specified for it. Signatures must be in same language and must tally.

    SIGNATURE IN CAPITAL LETTERS WILL RESULT IN REJECTION OF APPLICATION.


How to fill Information Sheet

*Incomplete/illegible applications will lead to rejection of applications.

**Please go through the Notification thoroughly and carefully and fill up the form adhering to the instructions quoted therein. However, the following guidelines will assist to fill up the Information Sheet.

Employment Notice No. CEN – 01/2010

  • Serial No.
    Application phase
    Details
  • 1.
    Information Sheet

    Please write the name as mentioned in Matriculation certificate in capital letters.

  • 2.
    Information Sheet

    Tick the appropriate box for Gender and Marital status.

  • 3.
    Information Sheet

    Write your Nationality.

  • 4.
    Information Sheet

    Kindly fill up the full address clearly indicating Village, Street No./House No. Tehsil, District, State as well as PIN CODE.

  • 5.
    Information Sheet

    Please write the details of DD/IPO whatever is attached in the fom of fee.

    Please write the Employment Notice No., Post applied for, Category Number, your name and Postal address on the reverse side of the DD/Front side of IPO in the space provided for it.

  • 6.
    Information Sheet

    Candidate should put tick mark (√) in the appropriate box provided.

  • 7.
    Information Sheet

    Copyright © Railway Recruitment Board Bilaspur 2009 All rights reserved

  • 8.
    Information Sheet

    Candidates who are Railway/Central/State/PSU employees should fill this. Such candidates should give all the details asked for in the form.

  • 9.
    Information Sheet

    Candidates who are Ex-Servicemen should fill this clearly giving all the details asked for in the form.

  • 10.
    Information Sheet

    This column should be filled up the candidates who seek age relaxation by ticking appropriate box given.

  • 11.
    Information Sheet

    Candidate, if debarred, should put tick mark (√) in the appropriate box.

  • 12.
    Information Sheet

    Candidate should put tick mark (√) in the relevant boxes provided for documents attached along with the application.

  • 13.
    Information Sheet

    Copy the sample paragraph in the space provided in your own handwriting in running hand. Non-copying will result in rejection.

    COPYING OF PARAGRAPH IN CAPITAL LETTERS WILL LEAD TO REJECTION.

    NON COPYING OF PARAGRAPH WILL ALSO LEAD TO REJECTION.

  • 14.
    Left hand thumb impression

    Please affix your Left Thumb Impression (LTI) in the space provided/specified for it, where the ridges must be clear and legible. LTI should not be blurred/smudged, dark or unreadable; else the application will be rejected.

  • 15.
    Signature

    Please put your full signature in running hand (NOT IN CAPITAL LETTERS) either in English or Hindi at the place specified for it. Signatures must be in same language and must tally.

    SIGNATURE IN CAPITAL LETTERS WILL RESULT IN REJECTION OF APPLICATION.
