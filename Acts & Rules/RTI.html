Right To Information - Act

The following specific information can be gathered from the respective web pages, as noted against each, of this web-site, besides others.

  1. How the selections are conducted - Selection procedure.
  2. Details of various forms being used - Forms Download.
  3. Frequently asked questions - FAQ
  4. Employment Notices indicating vacancy position, community wise breakup, eligibility criteria etc - Employment Notice Board.
  5. Position of receipt of applications with necessary information - Status of Applications.
  6. Details of eligible & rejection of applications - Status of Applications.
  7. Issue of call letters - Status of Applications.
  8. Date time and Venues of examination - Examination Schedule.
  9. Publication of results and panels - Results.

APPLICATION

A person who desires to obtain an information under the provision of Right to Information Act, 2005 may make a request in writing or through Electronic means in English or Hindi/Official language of Chhattisgarh, accompanying a fee of Rs.10/- (Rupees ten only), to-

    Public Information Officer & Secretary/Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur.
    Assistant Public Information Officer & Office Supdt./Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur, at the above noted address, specifying the desire information.

FEE

The above referred fee of Rs.10/- may be deposited in Cash & Pay Office, South East Central Railway, Bilaspur (C.G) against Allocation head No Z-652 or the same may be paid through DD (Demand Draft) or IPO (Indian Postal Order) or Bankers Cheque/Draft to be drawn in favour of Financial Adviser & Chief Accounts Officer, South East Central Railway, Bilaspur (C.G) payable at Bilaspur.

The person who seek information through Electronic means is also require to deposit the fee of Rs.10/- in the above shape and submit the Money receipt or DD or IPO in the office of RRB/Bilaspur.

AUTHORITIES

The details of Assistant Public Information Officer, Public Information Officer and Appellate Authority of this RRB are as under.

S. No. Designation Name Address

Note:-

'Electronic means' means using electronic equipment for the processing (including digital compression) and storage of data which is transmitted, conveyed and received by wire, by radio, by optical means or by other electromagnetic means.
